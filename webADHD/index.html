Understanding ADHD: Causes, Symptoms, and Treatments

What is ADHD?

Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is a neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by persistent patterns of inattention, impulsivity, and hyperactivity that interfere with daily functioning.

Causes of ADHD

The exact causes of ADHD are not known, but research suggests a combination of genetic, environmental, and neurological factors may contribute to its development.

Common Symptoms

Some common symptoms of ADHD include:

  • Inattention and difficulty focusing
  • Impulsivity and difficulty with self-control
  • Hyperactivity and restlessness

Treatment Options

Treatment for ADHD often involves a combination of behavioral therapy, medication, and support. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
